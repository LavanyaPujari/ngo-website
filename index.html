Support a Child's Future

Basti Ki Pathshala is on a mission to bring light and learning into the lives of underprivileged children living in marginalized communities. Your generous donation helps us provide books, stationery, nutritious meals, medical aid, and a safe environment where every child has the opportunity to grow and thrive.

Every contribution, big or small, makes a real difference. Together, we can ensure that no child is left behind in the journey of education and empowerment.
