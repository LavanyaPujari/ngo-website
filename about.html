About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala is a grassroots non-profit organization dedicated to empowering children in marginalized communities by offering access to quality education, life skills, and emotional development. We believe that education is a right, not a privilege, and aim to create safe, inclusive spaces where children can learn, grow, and dream without barriers. Our volunteers and supporters work tirelessly to ensure that every child receives the care and guidance they deserve, helping them build a brighter future.
